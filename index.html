How I Do Things:

1 //

Listen

We'll meet (face-to-face or online) to get clarity on who you are, who needs to know, and why they should care. Your vision and message function as my roadmap, and we'll get an idea of what I can do for you - whether that's design, site development, or web content.

2 //

Game Plan

I take everything I learned from our meeting and whip up a strategic plan aligned with your goals, focusing on brand consistency and meeting your project's requirements. I'll introduce a few service options, and we'll hammer out a timeline and compensation options together.

3 //

Magic

Once you approve the strategy, I go to work on your project, crafting a product that'll separate you from the competition and rock your socks right off. By telling your brand story digitally, we'll communicate your value, both enchanting and engaging your ideal audience.

4 //

Launch

After testing, I hand the project over to you for a final check-through. Once everything passes your inspection, the complete ownership of the product will be transferred to you. With development package purchases, you even get a complimentary training session to teach you how to manage your new site.
