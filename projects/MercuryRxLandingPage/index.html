What is Retrograde?

When a planet appears to be moving backwards, we say it is in "retrograde." The planet appears to be moving in reverse due to an optical illusion that occurs when one planet's orbit around the Sun outpaces another planet's.

The difference in speeds causes the retrograde planet to appear to stop and then move backwards from the Earth for a period of time even though it is still moving forward.

Astrologically speaking, retrogrades can bring:

  • issues from the past we thought we had dealt with
  • a pause in the house (area of life) the planet is transiting
  • a review of the ideals the planet symbolizes
  • an inability to harness the planet's energy the way normally would
  • a feeling of everything going wrong or moving backwards
  • an opportunity to take care of unfinished business

What is Mercury?

As the messenger of the gods, Mercury moves quickly. It is the fastest planet in the solar system and completes its orbit around the Sun in just 88 Earth days, spending between 14 and 30 days in each zodiac sign depending on the motion.

In astrology, Mercury represents our mind and how we assign intellectual meaning to all that we experience. It is said to govern:

  • Transportation & Electronics
  • Communication & Exchange of Ideas
  • Our Mental Faculties & Thought Processes
  • Data & Analysis
  • Organization & Synthesis

What is Mercury Retrograde?

Because Mercury has a tighter orbit around the Sun than the Earth does, it zips past the Earth three or four times each year – causing what we call Mercury Retrograde or Mercury Rx.

For most people, Mercury retrograde wreaks havoc on their electronics and travel plans. Disruptive issues surrounding communication are likely, and we can be confronted by ghosts of the past (including exes). Expect delays and lost items, and be extra careful when making major decisions or signing any contracts at this time as your mind may change once Mercury stations direct.

Many people born under a Mercury retrograde or with a detrimental Mercury note that they experience the retrograde effects while Mercury is direct and the direct effects during the retrograde.

Mercury in Pisces

Mutable Water

In Pisces, Mercury is in detriment. It is not able to operate as it normally does, so we experience a watering down of our thinking processes. Pisces is attuned the ethereal worlds of emotion, spirituality, and compassion – but its rose-colored glasses can cloud and obscure the facts. This transit leads to misunderstandings, so try to focus on communicating what you feel rather than what you think.

Blurred details, dissolution of facts, and the weight of the past makes black-and-white logical thinking all but impossible. What thought processes and "facts" do you cling to that could be dissolved or sacrificed for something better? Consider the possibilities and step away from the need to speak your truth perfectly.

Positives: dreamy, quiet, expansive, spiritual, ethereal, imaginative, colorful, mystical, friendly

Negatives: manipulative, victim-complex, naive, impractical, unrealistic, restrained, melancholy
