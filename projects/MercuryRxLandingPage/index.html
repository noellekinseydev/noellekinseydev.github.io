What is Retrograde?

When a planet appears to be moving backwards, we say it is in "retrograde." The planet appears to be moving in reverse due to an optical illusion that occurs when one planet's orbit around the Sun outpaces another planet's.

The difference in speeds causes the retrograde planet to appear to stop and then move backwards from the Earth for a period of time even though it is still moving forward.

Astrologically speaking, retrogrades can bring:

  • issues from the past we thought we had dealt with
  • a pause in the house (area of life) the planet is transiting
  • a review of the ideals the planet symbolizes
  • an inability to harness the planet's energy the way normally would
  • a feeling of everything going wrong or moving backwards
  • an opportunity to take care of unfinished business

What is Mercury?

As the messenger of the gods, Mercury moves quickly. It is the fastest planet in the solar system and completes its orbit around the Sun in just 88 Earth days, spending between 14 and 30 days in each zodiac sign depending on the motion.

In astrology, Mercury represents our mind and how we assign intellectual meaning to all that we experience. It is said to govern:

  • Transportation & Electronics
  • Communication & Exchange of Ideas
  • Our Mental Faculties & Thought Processes
  • Data & Analysis
  • Organization & Synthesis
